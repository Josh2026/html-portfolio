The Best Movies According to Joshua

My Top 5 Movies of all-time

The Gray Man

The movie had alot of great actions but was more than just action. Protecting those you love, sacrificing yourself for others... moments in the movie that really caught my eye.

Inception

This movie hit all the right spots. Love, action, drama. Also, I loved how it challenged my brain to be able to understand what was going on.

The Hunger Games Movie I

Really great to see lovers make it to the end.
